The Space Man

It was a routine space mission, or so I thought. As the engineer aboard the ship, I was responsible for ensuring that all systems were functioning properly. But something was off. The ship's controls were malfunctioning, and I couldn't figure out why. That's when I saw it - a strange figure lurking in the shadows.

At first, I thought it was just my imagination, but then I saw it move. The figure was humanoid, but its features were distorted and twisted. I tried to radio for help, but my signal was being jammed.

The figure approached me, its steps slow and deliberate. I tried to back away, but I was trapped. The figure reached out and touched me, and I felt a jolt of electricity surge through my body.

That's when I realized the horrifying truth - the figure was not a creature, but a space man, an alien life form that had infiltrated our ship. It was trying to take control of our technology and use it for its own purposes.

As the space man advanced towards me, I knew I had to act fast. I grabbed the nearest tool I could find and swung it at the creature, hoping to fend it off. But it was too strong, and it overpowered me with ease.

Now, I'm trapped aboard this ship, alone with the space man. I can hear it moving in the shadows, plotting its next move. I don't know how much time I have left before it takes control of the ship completely. All I can do is wait, and hope that someone will come to my rescue before it's too late.
